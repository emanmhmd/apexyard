Takbeer al-Tashreeq

Dhul Hijjah 9-13 · After every fard prayer

اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَاللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ وَلِلَّهِ الْحَمْدُ

Allah is the Greatest, Allah is the Greatest. None has the right to be worshipped except Allah. Allah is the Greatest, Allah is the Greatest, and all praise is due to Allah.

Ibn ʿUmar and Abu Hurayrah would proclaim Takbeer aloud in the markets during these days. — Bukhāri

Sayyid al-Istighfar

O Allah, You are my Lord, none has the right to be worshipped except You. You created me and I am Your servant... If said in the morning with certainty and one dies that day before evening — they are from the people of Jannah. — Bukhari
